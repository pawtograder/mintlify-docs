## Overview

The assignment dashboard provides instructors with a comprehensive view of assignment progress, submission status, and grading metrics.

## Dashboard sections

### Submission overview
- Total submissions received
- Pending submissions
- Graded vs. ungraded submissions
- Average submission time

### Grading progress
- Number of submissions graded
- Average grading time
- Graders assigned and their progress
- Pending review assignments

### Performance metrics
- Average score
- Score distribution
- Common errors and issues
- Test pass rates

### Student engagement
- Students who have submitted
- Students who haven't submitted
- Late submissions
- Extension requests

## Best practices

- Check the dashboard regularly to monitor assignment progress
- Use metrics to identify students who may need help
- Track grading progress to ensure timely feedback
- Export data for further analysis or record-keeping

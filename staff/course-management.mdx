# Course Management

## Exporting Course Data

Pawtograder provides export functionality for course rosters and group information.

### Student Roster Export

Export your course roster to CSV format with student names and email addresses. This is useful for:

- Sharing student lists with external systems
- Creating mailing lists
- Generating reports for administrative purposes

The exported CSV includes:
- Student names in "Name <email>" format
- All enrolled students in the course

### Group Export

Export group assignments to CSV format with detailed information about group composition and mentors. The export includes:

- **GroupName**: The name of each group
- **StudentNames**: All students in the group in "Name <email>" format
- **MentorName**: The assigned mentor/grader in "Name <email>" format (if applicable)

The CSV export includes:
- UTF-8 BOM for proper character encoding
- Formula-safe CSV escaping to prevent injection attacks
- Sorted rows for stable, consistent exports

This export is particularly useful for:
- Tracking group assignments across multiple projects
- Coordinating with mentors or graders
- Generating reports on group composition
- Importing group data into external systems

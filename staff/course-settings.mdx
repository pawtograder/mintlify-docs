# Course Settings

Course settings allow instructors to configure global preferences and policies for their course.

## Time Zone Configuration

Pawtograder supports configurable time zones for your course. The time zone setting determines how due dates, survey deadlines, and other time-based features are displayed and interpreted.

**Default Time Zone**: America/New_York (Eastern Time)

To change your course's time zone:

1. Navigate to Course Settings
2. Select your preferred time zone from the dropdown
3. Save your changes

All existing and future deadlines will be displayed in the configured time zone. This ensures consistency across your course, especially for courses with students or staff in different geographic locations.

<Note>
Admin views also have a default time zone setting to ensure consistent time display across administrative interfaces.
</Note>

## Late Token Policy

Configure the number of late tokens available to students and how they can be used for assignment extensions. See the [Late Tokens](/students/assignments/late-tokens) documentation for more details on how students use these tokens.

## Grading Conflicts

Manage conflicts of interest for graders to ensure fair and unbiased grading. Graders can self-report conflicts, and instructors can review and manage all conflicts for the course.

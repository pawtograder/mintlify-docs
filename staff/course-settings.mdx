# Course Settings

## Timezone Configuration

Pawtograder allows you to configure a default timezone for your course. This timezone is used to display all dates and times throughout the platform, including assignment due dates, survey deadlines, and poll schedules.

To configure your course timezone:

1. Navigate to Course Settings
2. Select your preferred timezone from the dropdown menu
3. Save your changes

All dates and times will be displayed in the configured timezone for course staff. Students will see dates and times in their local timezone by default, but the system uses your configured course timezone for all deadline calculations.

The default timezone is America/New_York (Eastern Time).

## Late Token Policy

Configure how many late tokens students receive and how they can be used for assignment extensions. See the [Late Tokens](/students/assignments/late-tokens) documentation for more details on how students use late tokens.

## Grading Conflicts

Course staff can declare conflicts of interest to ensure they don't grade assignments where conflicts exist. See the [Assigning Grading Tasks](/staff/assignments/grading-assignments) documentation for more details.

# Instructor Dashboard

The instructor dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your course's grading status, student engagement, and system health. Access it from the course navigation menu.

## Assignment Grading Overview

The dashboard displays all assignments with key metrics organized into three sections:

### Needs Attention

Assignments with incomplete work, open regrade requests, or other items requiring action:

- **Submission Reviews**: Count of incomplete vs. total submission reviews
- **Review Assignments**: Individual grading tasks assigned to staff
- **Grades Released**: How many grades have been released to students
- **Regrades/Extensions**: Open regrade requests and active due date extensions

### Upcoming with No Blocking Work

Assignments with upcoming due dates that have no pending grading or issues.

### Complete or Stable

Past-due or undated assignments where all grading is complete and no issues remain.

## Dashboard Metrics

For each assignment, you can see:

- **Grading Due**: The due date for grading work (in your course timezone)
- **Submitted Students**: How many students have submitted vs. total enrollment
- **Submission Reviews**: Completion status of overall submission grading
- **Review Assignments**: Individual grading tasks assigned to staff members
- **Grades Released**: Whether grades have been released (fully, partially, or not at all)
- **Regrades/Extensions**: Open regrade requests and students with active extensions

### Understanding Review Metrics

- **Submission Reviews**: One per submission, tracks overall completion of grading for that submission
- **Review Assignments**: Individual tasks assigned to graders. Multiple graders can be assigned to review different parts of the same submission, so review assignment counts may exceed submission review counts

## Quick Actions

Each assignment row provides links to:

- **Grading Dashboard**: Detailed grading progress and rubric completion
- **Submissions**: View all student submissions
- **Regrade Requests**: Manage student regrade requests
- **Due Date Exceptions**: View and manage extensions

## Summary Cards

At the top of the dashboard, summary cards show:

- **Assignments Needing Attention**: Count of assignments with incomplete work
- **Incomplete Submission Reviews**: Total across all assignments
- **Incomplete Review Assignments**: Total grading tasks pending
- **Grade Release Status**: How many assignments have fully released grades
- **Open Regrade Requests**: Total pending regrade requests

## Additional Dashboard Sections

### Discussion Activity

View recent discussion board activity and engagement metrics.

### Workflow Runs

Monitor autograder and system workflow execution:

- **Last Hour**: Recent workflow runs and error counts
- **Last 24 Hours**: Daily workflow statistics
- **Recent Errors**: Quick access to recent workflow failures

### Help Queue

See current office hours activity and help request status.

# Extensions

Pawtograder provides three mechanisms for letting a student submit past
the original due date: student-spent **late tokens**, instructor-granted
**manual extensions**, and instructor-**gifted tokens**. All three are
managed from the assignment's **Due Date Exceptions** page.

For the class-wide token allotment per assignment, see
[Configuration → Late tokens](/staff/assignments/configuration#late-tokens).

## Late tokens

When the assignment allows late tokens, each enrolled student can spend
one token per 24 hours of extension on their own due date.

- Token requests are recorded in the student's submission history.
- Group assignments treat tokens at the group level — one token covers
  the whole group's deadline.
- A student's remaining token balance is shown on their assignments page.

### When tokens are applied

Each assignment chooses *when* students spend tokens, via the **Require
students to apply late tokens before the original due date** checkbox in
[Configuration → Late tokens](/staff/assignments/configuration#late-tokens):

- **Checked (default)** — manual mode. Students must apply tokens
  **before** the original due date to extend it.
- **Unchecked** — auto-apply mode. When a student submits after the
  deadline, the required tokens are deducted automatically and the
  deadline is extended to cover the submission. Tokens are computed per
  24-hour period (rounded up) and deducted atomically and idempotently
  (resubmitting within the same window does not spend extra tokens). If
  the student has insufficient tokens, the late submission is rejected
  with a clear error.

Auto-apply mode requires **Max Late Tokens** to be at least 1; the
assignment settings form enforces this.

## Granting a manual extension

From **Due Date Exceptions**, click **Add Exception** and choose the
student (or group, for group assignments). The form accepts either:

- A **Target Due Date** (date picker) — choose the new deadline directly.
- **Hours Extended** and **Minutes Extended** — extend by a duration
  rather than choosing an end time.

Use **Tokens to Consume** if you want the extension to be billed
against the student's late-token balance, or leave it at 0 for a free
extension. The **Notes** field is internal and helps future-you
remember why the exception was granted.

For group assignments, the exception applies to every member of the
group automatically.

## Gifting late tokens

Gifted tokens are added to a student's available balance and are
spendable like any other token. Use this to compensate for special
circumstances (illness, accommodation, transit issues) without picking
a specific new deadline. The gifted tokens stack on top of the
class-wide allocation.

## Self-review deadline shift

When an assignment's due date is extended — via a token or a manual
exception — Pawtograder shifts the affected student's self-review
deadline by the same amount, so they retain the normal review window
after submitting. No manual action is required.

## Bulk review-due-date updates

The **Grading Assignments** page lets you adjust *grading* and
*self-review* review due dates in bulk for a section, a rubric, or a
set of selected rows. See
[Bulk Actions](/staff/assignments/bulk-actions#bulk-review-due-date-updates)
for the procedure.

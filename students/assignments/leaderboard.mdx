# Assignment Leaderboard

Some assignments have a **Leaderboard** tab. If you don't see one, it isn't enabled for that assignment.

## What you see

The leaderboard lists ranked entries based on the autograder score of each student's (or group's) active submission. Your own row is highlighted so you can quickly find it in a long list.

Ties are broken by submission time: earlier submissions rank higher.

Rankings update as new submissions come in.

## Identity and visibility

What others see about you on the leaderboard depends on your instructor's configuration:

- **Public**: real names are shown
- **Anonymized**: pseudonyms are shown instead of names
- **Personal-only**: you see only your own rank and score

You can always see your own rank, regardless of mode.

<Note>
Your leaderboard position does not affect your grade. It's a view of submission scores; nothing more.
</Note>

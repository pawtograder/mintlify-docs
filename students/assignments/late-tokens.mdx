# Late Tokens

Pawtograder supports late submission policies based on "late tokens." Instructors determine how many late tokens are available to each student, and how many tokens can be used for each late submission. Each token extends the deadline by 24 hours, with no penalty.

The exact behavior depends on how your instructor has configured the assignment:

- **Tokens must be applied before the deadline** (default): You must use the **Extend Due Date** button before the original due date passes. Once the deadline has passed, you can no longer apply tokens for that assignment.
- **Tokens are applied automatically on late submission**: If your instructor has enabled this option, you can submit after the deadline and a late token will be automatically deducted from your balance. You will see a notice on the assignment page indicating this policy.

<Warning>
Check the assignment page to understand which policy applies. If tokens must be applied before the deadline, you cannot submit late without first extending your due date.
</Warning>

## Automatic Late Token Application

Some assignments allow late tokens to be applied automatically when you submit after the deadline. When this policy is enabled:

- The assignment page shows a notice: *"Deadline passed: submitting will auto-apply a late token if you have one remaining."*
- When you push a commit after the deadline, Pawtograder automatically deducts the required number of tokens from your balance and extends your due date to cover the submission.
- If you do not have enough tokens remaining, your submission will be rejected with a message explaining how many tokens are needed.
- Submitting the same commit again (e.g. after a retry) will not deduct additional tokens.

<Note>
Automatic token application requires that you have sufficient tokens remaining. If you are close to your token limit, check your balance before submitting late.
</Note>

## Gifted Late Tokens

Instructors can gift additional late tokens to individual students beyond the class-wide allocation, on a case-by-case basis. Gifted tokens:

- Are automatically added to your available token balance
- Work exactly like your original tokens—each extends a deadline by 24 hours and must be applied before your current due date
- Display in your remaining token count (which always reflects both your initial tokens and any gifted tokens)
- Can be used even if you've exhausted your initial allocation
- Are tracked separately in the system (they appear as negative values internally) for instructor visibility

For example, if you've used all 4 of your initial tokens but your instructor gifts you 1 additional token, you'll see 1 remaining token available to use.

## Late Tokens and Group Assignments

The semantics of late tokens for group assignments are intended to be as straightforward and fair as possible:

- Any student in the group can apply a late token as long as they have one available (and the maximum number of tokens hasn't yet been applied to the assignment). It does not matter if a groupmate is out of tokens.
- All group members will have a token deducted from their total.
- All group members will see the same due date.

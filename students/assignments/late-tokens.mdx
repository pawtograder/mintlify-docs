# Late Tokens

Pawtograder supports late submission policies based on "late tokens." Instructors determine how many late tokens are available to each student, and how many tokens can be used for each late submission. Each token extends the deadline by 24 hours, with no penalty. **Late tokens must be applied before the deadline.** If it is permitted to utilize multiple late tokens, you can apply them all at once, or one at a time, but must always apply them before the updated deadline.

Instructors can also gift additional late tokens to individual students beyond the class-wide allocation. Gifted tokens work exactly like regular tokens and are included in your remaining token count.

On each assignment page where late tokens can be applied, you will see an "Extend Due Date" button next to your due date.

## Gifted Late Tokens

Instructors can gift additional late tokens to individual students beyond the class-wide allocation. These gifted tokens:

- Add to your total available late tokens
- Display correctly in your remaining token count
- Can be used just like your original tokens
- Are tracked separately in the system for instructor visibility

If you receive gifted tokens after using all your original tokens, you will see the updated count and be able to apply extensions again.

Instructors can gift additional late tokens to students on a case-by-case basis. These gifted tokens are added to your available balance and work exactly like your original allocation. The system correctly tracks both your initial tokens and any gifted tokens, showing you the accurate remaining balance when you apply extensions.

Your instructor may gift you additional late tokens beyond the class-wide allocation. These gifted tokens:

- Are automatically added to your available token balance
- Work exactly like your regular late tokens
- Display in your remaining token count
- Can be used even if you've exhausted your initial allocation

If you receive gifted tokens, you'll see them reflected in your total available tokens on the assignment page.

## Gifted late tokens

Instructors can gift additional late tokens to students beyond their initial allocation. These gifted tokens work exactly like regular late tokens and are automatically included in your remaining token count. If you've used all your initial tokens but received gifted tokens from your instructor, you'll be able to apply those tokens to extend deadlines.

## Late tokens and group assignments

The semantics of late tokens for group assignments are intended to be as straightforward and fair as possible:

- Any student in the group can apply a late token as long as they have one available (and the maximum number of tokens hasn't yet been applied to the assignment). It does not matter if a groupmate is out of tokens.
- All group members will have a token deducted from their total.
- All group members will see the same due date.
